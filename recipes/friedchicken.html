Crispy fried chicken

allrecipes, crispy-fried-chicken

Time breakdown:

  • Prep Time: 15 Mins
  • Cook Time: 35 mins
  • Additional Time: 30 mins
  • Total time: 1 hr 20 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer

  2. Put the flour in a large plastic bag "(let the amount of chicken you are cooking dictate the amount of flour you use).
    Season the flour with paprika, salt and pepper to taste(paprika helps to brown the chicken).

  3. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk, then a few at a time, put them in a bag with the flour, seal the bag, and shake to coat well.

  4. Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. Let sit until flour is of a paste-like consistency. This is crucial!

  5. Fill a large skillet (cast iron is best) about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil. Heat until VERY hot.

  6. Put in as many chicken pieces as the skilled can hold. Brown the chicken in hot oil on both sides.

  7. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy).
    Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy.

  8. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels. Depending on how much chicken you have, you may have to fry in a few shifts. Keep the chicken finished in a slightly warm oven while preparing the rest.
